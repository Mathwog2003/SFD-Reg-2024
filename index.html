Lets Celebrate Software Freedom day

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is a worldwide celebration of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). Our goal in this celebration which remembers the benefits of free and open software and promotes its use. Every third Saturday of September, this date is celebrated by thousands of people all around the planet, who reminds us the importance of sharing knowledge in computing field. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use.

Who Are We ?

Cuddalore GLUG is a non-for-profit organization and a social movement which aims at enlightening the masses in Free Software, Openness, Free Culture and Knowledge Freedom. We believe in a world where every child can read. Our mission is to invest in early childhood education in order to empower the next generation. We do that, by creating educational programs and providing necessary resources in underprivileged areas. We believe in smart fund allocation, and therefore we employ minimal staff.
